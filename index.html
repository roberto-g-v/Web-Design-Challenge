Summary: Latitude vs. X

X = Temperature, Cloudiness, Wind Speed or Humidity.

In this example, I have created a Python script to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. To accomplish this, I've used a simple Python library, the OpenWeatherMap API, and a little common sense to create a representative model of weather across world cities.

With the information gathered, I've used Pandas library to organize, clean, analize the information. Also, with Pandas, I ploted the information in different scatter plots to understand the relationship between the diferente variables.

Hypotesis to be proven:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
